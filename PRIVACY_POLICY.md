# Privacy Policy for Vanguard Shield

**Last Updated: April 22, 2026**

Vanguard Shield ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information Collection and Use
Vanguard Shield is designed with a **Privacy-First** approach. 
- **No Personal Data:** We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, or contacts.
- **No File Uploads:** When you scan a file, the actual file content **never** leaves your device. We only generate a cryptographic SHA-256 hash (a digital fingerprint) of the file to check against our threat database.

## 2. Permissions
To provide security services, the app requires the following permissions:
- **Storage/File Access:** Needed only to allow you to select a file for scanning.
- **Internet Access:** Necessary to query the latest threat signatures from our secure Firebase Cloud database.

## 3. Data Security
Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data stored on our servers. The communication between the app and our threat database is encrypted via Secure Socket Layer (SSL).

## 4. Third-Party Services
We use **Google Firebase** for our real-time threat database and **Google AdMob** for displaying advertisements. These third-party services may process non-personal device identifiers as per their own privacy policies.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via GitHub Issues.
