# Privacy Policy for Vanguard Shield

**Last Updated:** April 24, 2026

---

## 📝 Introduction
Vanguard Shield ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy[cite: 2]. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application and outlines our limitations of liability[cite: 2].

---

## 🔍 1. Information Collection and Use
Vanguard Shield is designed with a **Privacy-First** approach[cite: 2].

*   **No Personal Data:** We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, or contacts[cite: 2].
*   **No File Uploads:** When you scan a file, the actual file content **never** leaves your device[cite: 2]. We only generate a cryptographic SHA-256 hash (a digital fingerprint) of the file to check against our threat database[cite: 2].

---

## 🔑 2. Permissions
To provide security services, the app requires the following permissions[cite: 2]:

*   **Storage/File Access:** Needed only to allow you to select a file for scanning purposes[cite: 2].
*   **Internet Access:** Necessary to query the latest threat signatures from our secure Firebase Cloud database[cite: 2].

---

## 🛡️ 3. Data Security
*   Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data stored on our servers[cite: 2].
*   The communication between the app and our threat database is encrypted via Secure Socket Layer (SSL)[cite: 2].

---

## 🤝 4. Third-Party Services
We use **Google Firebase** for our real-time threat database and **Google AdMob** for displaying advertisements[cite: 2]. These third-party services may process non-personal device identifiers (such as Advertising IDs) according to their own privacy policies[cite: 2].
